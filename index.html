Who We Are

Africa's Cleanest Energy Partner

Founded in 2009, SolarNova has grown into Zambia's most trusted solar energy company. We combine German engineering, African expertise, and genuine passion for a cleaner planet.

From humble beginnings in Lusaka, we now serve clients across 8 provinces — helping homes, businesses and communities break free from grid dependence.

🏆 ISO 9001 Certified 🌍 ZEMA Registered ⚡ ZESCO Partner
🎯

Our Mission

Make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every Zambian household and business.

🔭

Our Vision

A Zambia powered 100% by renewable energy by 2040 — prosperous, green and energy-secure.

🤝

Why Choose Us

Certified installers, 10-year warranty, 24/7 support, and the lowest cost-per-watt guarantee.
