Bright Nights, Zero Running Costs

Our all-in-one solar street lights integrate the solar panel, LED lamp, lithium battery and controller into one compact unit. No trenching, no cabling, no ZESCO account needed. SolarNova has installed over 5,000 solar street lights across 6 provinces.

Panel, battery, LED and controller integrated in one sleek unit. Installed in under 2 hours per pole.
