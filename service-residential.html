Residential Solar Installation

Custom home solar systems designed to slash your ZESCO bills and keep your lights on 24/7. SolarNova has powered over 1,800 homes across Zambia.

How It Works

Your Home, Fully Solar-Powered

Our residential solar process is completely end-to-end. A certified engineer visits, analyses your electricity usage, maps your roof's solar potential, and designs a system tailored to your needs and budget.

We handle ZESCO net metering registration, all permits, and provide a 10-year workmanship warranty. Our app lets you track generation and savings in real time.
